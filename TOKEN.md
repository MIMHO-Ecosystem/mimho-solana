## Token Purpose

MIMHO Solana is the meme/community launch side of the broader MIMHO ecosystem.

The Solana launch is designed to support:

- community growth
- meme culture
- social distribution
- public visibility
- cross-chain ecosystem awareness

## Important Notice

The initial Solana token launch does not automatically activate BSC/EVM modules.

The following modules are separate and belong to the broader MIMHO ecosystem roadmap:

- staking
- vesting
- DAO
- marketplace
- locker
- bridge
- cross-chain utility
- rewards

These modules will only be considered active when officially deployed, tested and documented.
