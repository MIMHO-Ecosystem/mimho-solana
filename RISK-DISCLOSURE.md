# MIMHO Risk Disclosure

## Important Notice

MIMHO is a meme coin ecosystem.

Participation in meme coins, crypto assets and launchpad tokens is highly risky.

Users should only participate after understanding the risks.

## No Profit Guarantee

MIMHO does not guarantee:

- profit
- price increase
- liquidity
- exchange listing
- migration
- long-term value
- market demand
- community growth
- staking rewards
- bridge availability

The token price may fall significantly or go to zero.

## Launchpad Risk

The initial Solana launch is planned through a standard public launchpad flow.

Launchpad participation may involve:

- high volatility
- failed bonding curve completion
- liquidity uncertainty
- bot activity
- slippage
- fake tokens
- fake links
- impersonation scams

Users must verify official links before interacting.

## Migration Risk

If the bonding curve completes, any migration will follow the launch platform's supported path available at that time.

No specific AMM migration is guaranteed.

No Raydium, PumpSwap or other liquidity destination should be considered guaranteed unless officially confirmed after launch.

## Cross-Chain Risk

MIMHO is designed as a cross-chain ecosystem, but cross-chain modules are released in phases.

There is currently no guarantee of:

- active bridge
- active cross-chain transfer
- active Solana-BSC synchronization
- active cross-chain staking
- active cross-chain rewards

Any future bridge or cross-chain mechanism must be separately tested, deployed and documented.

## Smart Contract Risk

The initial Solana launch does not use a custom Solana program.

The BSC/EVM V2 contracts are maintained separately and are under security hardening.

Smart contracts can contain bugs, logic errors or vulnerabilities.

Users should not assume any contract is risk-free.

## Market Risk

Crypto markets are volatile.

Meme coins are especially volatile.

Users may lose part or all of their funds.

## Scam Risk

Users should be careful with:

- fake websites
- fake mint addresses
- fake Telegram groups
- fake X/Twitter accounts
- fake GitHub repositories
- fake airdrops
- fake staking links
- fake bridge links
- fake support accounts

Official links should be verified through this repository and official MIMHO communication channels.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for their own decisions.

MIMHO does not provide financial advice.

Nothing in this repository should be interpreted as investment advice.

## Summary

Participating in MIMHO or any meme coin involves high risk.

Only participate with funds you can afford to lose.
